GORILLA

Gorillas are ground-dwelling,predominantly herbivorous apes that inhabit the forest of central Sub-Saharan Africa.The genus Gorilla is divided into species:the eastern gorillas and the western gorillas(both critically endangered).

They are the largest livingprimates.The DNA OF gorillas is highly similar to that of humans,from 95 to 99% depending on what is included and they are the next closet living relatives to humans after the chimpanzees and bonobos.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Habit: Gorillas' natural habitats cover tropical or subtropical forest in Sub-Saharan Africa.The mountain gorilla inhabits the Albertine Rift montane cloud forests of the Volcanoes.
