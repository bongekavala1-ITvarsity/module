TIGER

Tiger is the largest extant cat species and a memberof the genus panthera,it is mos recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange brown fur with a lighter underside.It is an ape predator , primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and wild boar.

It is territorial and generally a solitary but social predator,requiring large contiguous areas of habitat , which support its requirements for prey and rearing of its offspring

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Tigers are found in amazingly diverse habitats:rain forests,grasslands ,savannas and even mangrove swamps
