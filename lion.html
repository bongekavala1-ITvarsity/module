LION

Lion is species in the family Felidae and a member of the genus Panthera

It has a muscular,deep-chested body,short, rounded head, round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.it is sexually dimorphic;adult male lions have a prominent mane.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

lions live in a variety of habitats but prefer grassland,savanna, dense scrub, and open woodland..
