PANDA

Panda is a bear native to south central China. It is characterised by large,black patches around its eye,over the ears, and across

its round body.

The name name "giant panda"is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda, a neighboring mudteloid.Though it belongs to the order Carnivora, the giant panda is a folivore,with bamboo shoots and leaves making up more than 99% of its diet.
