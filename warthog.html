WARTHOG

Warthog is a genus in the family Suidae, it is the sole genus of subfamily Phacochoerinae. They are pigs who live in open and semi-open habitats, even in quite arid regions, in sub-Saharan Africa. The two species were formerly considered conspecific under the scientific name Phacochoerus aethiopicus,but today this is limited to the desert warthog

The head of the common warthog is large , with a mane down the spine to the middle of the back,spares hair covers the body .it is usuallyblack or brown.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

they can be found in grassland, savanna and woodland in sub-suharan Africa.
